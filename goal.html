Use the financial tune-up calculator in your first session with a customer as a fun yet productive assessment. The calculator give you a quick and accurate snapshot of your customer’s current financial standing and provides insights into savings and financial goals.

Action-driven goals have three essential components:

ASSET-ORIENTED

Customers often identify goals like "getting out of debt." Help refocus their attention on the reasons they want to get out of debt. The answers will lead to asset-oriented goals, such as applying for U.S. citizenship, remodeling a room in their house, or establishing an emergency savings fund.

FORWARD-THINKING

Help your customers target positive forward-thinking goals like "buying a new home," and guide their attention away from common backward-thinking goals, such as "cleaning up bad credit." The bad credit is an obstacle to their goals, and the credit "clean-up" is a strategy to achieve that goal.

PASSIONATELY HELD

Commitment to achieving one's goals takes passion and sacrifice. Customers are more likely to stick to a strategic plan if they feel passionately about their financial goals.
