Assets

Outcome Save Consistantly

Overview

Through financial coaching you empower customers to build, organize, and take control of their financial lives by making informed financial choices. Regardless of your customers’ current financial situation, building assets is vital to achieve financial security. Research has shown that even people who have very low income can save and build assets. Assets range from bank and retirement accounts, to insurance and public-sponsored safety nets like food stamps. Not all of these will be appropriate for all customers, but it is essential that every customer saves consistently for an action-driven goal, even if the amount is small. Remember savings is an activity, not an amount. Your goal here is to remind customers it’s not important how much you save; it’s that you establish a regular savings habit.
